THE LEGEND OF KR2

Core features of game:

  • Immersive Dystopian World: Experience a gritty setting with over 50 unique moves.
  • Deep Character Interaction: Engage with over 50 interactive NPCs.
  • Expansive Content: 70 side quests and a 35-chapter main storyline.
  • Psychological Dark Adventure: Brutal narrative with high-fidelity visuals.
  • Dynamic Choice System: Six distinct outcomes based on your decisions.
  • Karma Mechanics: Reputation system based on moral choices.
  • Advanced Crafting: Build Melee, Ranged, and Alien weaponry.
  • Weapon Fusion: Combine types to create devastating hybrids.
  • Hero Abilities: Integrate weaponry with unlockable abilities.
